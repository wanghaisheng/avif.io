## Introduction to Emulsion and AVIF

In the age of data and countless image formats, a lightweight, fast, and user-friendly image viewer is vital. Enter Emulsion, an image viewer developed primarily by Artúr Kovács and designed to elevate the image viewing experience on Windows, Mac, and Linux platforms. With Emulsion, users enjoy exceptional speed and minimalist design, quick access to zoom features, and a choice between dark and light interfaces for improved usability.

As technology continues to advance, so do the needs of image enthusiasts. In comes AVIF or AV1 Image File Format - a high-quality, open-source, and royalty-free image specification that provides better compression efficiency and improved detail preservation. By including AVIF support in Emulsion, the software showcases its commitment to providing superior performance and quality.

## A Closer Look at AVIF

AVIF was designed to offer a modern, efficient alternative to established image formats, like JPEG. Developed by the Alliance for Open Media and first finalized in February 2019, AVIF offers a range of color spaces, HDR and SDR capability, and both lossless and lossy compression options. Compared to the popular HEIC (High Efficiency Image Format), AVIF uses the AV1 codec to compress images while HEIC relies on HEVC.

Here are some noteworthy features of AVIF:

- Improved detail preservation, fewer artifacts, and less color bleeding.
- Support for multiple color spaces and signaling, including PQ, HLG, sRGB, BT.709, BT.601, and wide color gamut spaces.
- Options for 8-, 10-, and 12-bit color depths in both monochrome and multi-component formats.
- Versatility in compression methods, such as 4:2:0, 4:2:2, 4:4:4 chroma subsampling, and RGB.
- Capability for film grain synthesis, image sequences, and animations.
- Compatibility with distinct hardware through two AVIF profiles: Baseline and Advanced.

So, how does this benefit Emulsion users? By incorporating AVIF support, Emulsion ensures that image enthusiasts enjoy enriched detail and color in their images, along with faster loading times for an even more immersive experience.

## Emulsion's Commitment to Excellence

In August 2020, a GitHub user requested AVIF support for Emulsion. The development team was quick to respond and incorporate this specification into the build process, demonstrating attentiveness to user needs and dedication to the software's improvement.

Emulsion not only offers excellent image viewing but also embraces cutting-edge technology, making it an invaluable tool for image lovers.
